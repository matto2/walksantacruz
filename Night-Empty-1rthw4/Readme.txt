HOW TO ACCESS ALTERNATE AND LIGATURE IN ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR/PHOTOSHOP

Open glyphs panel :

ADOBE PHOTOSHOP
go to Window - Glyphs

ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR
go to Window - Type - Glyphs
